<font size =4 color="black">Priorities</font>
<br>
<font color="red" size=4>Knowledge Sharing</font>
<br>
Provide ready access to innovative resources for educators, researchers, community members, leaders and professional organizations.<br>
Mobilize and translate research to inform policy, professional learning and best practices.
<br><br> 
<font color="red" size=4>Relationship building</font>
<br>
Support collaborative research efforts to identify mutual research interests
and funding opportunities.
<br>
Bring together education researchers and practioners through inperson and
online forum.
<br>
Connect researchers with graduate and undergraduate students.
<br>
Facilitate the "discovery to delivery" arc of innovative products and services
resulting from research.
